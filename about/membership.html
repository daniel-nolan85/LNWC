Become a Member of NCWF and LNWC

As a member, along with fellowship with others dedicated to enhancement of our natural environment around the Lake Norman area, you will be notified and given priority to participate in:

  • Significant environmental conservation projects
  • Environmental educational opportunities
  • Educational and "fun filled" field trips
  • Programs and events that are appealing to the entire family
  • Educational lecture series
  • Priority booking and/or discounts to events and programs

As part of the LNWC membership you will automatically become a member of the North Carolina Wildlife Federation along with all its programs and opportunities. You'll also receive NCWF's Quarterly Journal and Annual Report.

LNWC is a 501c3 Tax-deductible organization.

For more information email us at info@LNWC.org
