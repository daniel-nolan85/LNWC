Robbins Park Nature Preserve

LNWC has joined with the Cornelius Park and Recreation Department (PARC) to help develop the nature preserve at Robbins Park. We have identified Earth Day as a target date for mass plantings. Therefore, this winter, volunteers will be sought to help prepare the site. Keep an eye out for your email asking for park volunteers! Postings will also be placed on our website.
