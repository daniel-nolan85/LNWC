Volunteer Opportunities

LNWC has many volunteer opportunities throughout the year. Some are ongoing, while others only occur periodically. Follow us on our website, Facebook, and through email notifications to find out about these opportunities.

Below find several volunteer opportunities and more information about each of them. If you have any additional questions or would like to sign up for any of these, please contact us at info@LNWC.org or follow the link provided under each activity.

Robbins Park

Osprey/Heron Platforms

Several times a year we need to build, install and/or replace Osprey/Heron Nesting Platforms. If you would be interested in volunteering follow us on our website, Facebook, Twitter or Meetup. Contact us at info@LNWC.org if interested.

Birdhouse Build

Periodically we have a need to build and/or install birdhouses. If you are interested please contact us at info@LNWC.org.

Island Habitat

The Island Habitat project helps enhance island habitats for wildlife by cleaning them of refuse, by erecting nesting boxes, and stabilizing shorelines to prevent island erosion.
